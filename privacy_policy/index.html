4. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal Information You Disclose to Us

In Short: We collect personal information that you provide to us.

We collect personal information when you:

  • Register on the Airmass Xpress mobile application;
  • Participate in activities within the mobile application;
  • Contact us through any means.

Types of Personal Data Collected

  • Contact Data: Physical address, email address, and phone numbers.
  • Financial Data: Mobile wallet number (transmitted to our payment service provider; not stored by us).
  • Identity Data: First and last name, username, title, date of birth, and gender.
  • Marketing and Communications Data: Preferences in receiving marketing material and communications.
  • Profile Data: Username, password, interests, preferences, bids, and feedback.
  • Transaction Data: Payment records and services hired through the app.
  • Company Data: Company name, address, and proof of registration (for service providers).

Sensitive Information

We do not collect sensitive personal data (e.g. race, religion, health, marital status, or biometric data).

Application Data

  • Geolocation: Location access (with user consent) for location-based services.
  • Device Access: Camera, storage, and gallery access (with user consent).
  • Push Notifications: Sent for account and app activity (users can opt out via settings).

This information is used to maintain application functionality, troubleshoot issues, and for internal analytics.

In Short: We process your information to provide and improve our Services, ensure security, and comply with the law.

Legal Bases for Processing

  • Performance of a contract: Required for service delivery.
  • Legitimate interests: To improve services and prevent fraud.
  • Consent: For marketing or optional services.
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable law or regulation.

Purposes and Types of Data We Process

We process your personal data for the following purposes and use the associated types of data:

  • To create and manage your account → Identity Data, Contact Data, Profile Data
  • To deliver services and facilitate usage of the mobile application → Contact Data, Transaction Data
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries → Contact Data, Profile Data
  • To send you important administrative messages (e.g. password resets, service updates) → Contact Data

  • To improve services, products, and the overall user experience → Usage Data, Technical Data
  • To verify companies registering to offer services via Airmass Xpress → Company Data

6. WHEN AND WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION?

In Short: We share data only when necessary, with trusted providers or under specific conditions.

  • Google Maps API: Used to estimate location using GPS, Wi-Fi, or mobile towers. Data is cached on your device for up to 6 months.
  • Other Users: Public posts (e.g., job requests, profile info) may be visible to others on the platform.
  • Payment Service Providers: Mobile wallet numbers and payment info are shared for secure transactions.
  • Third-party service providers: Vendors may support services like cloud storage, analytics, or customer support, under data protection agreements.

We retain your information only as long as needed to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless required otherwise by law.
